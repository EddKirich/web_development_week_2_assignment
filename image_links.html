Here's a fun fact about technology:
Did you know that the first computer mouse was made of wood? Invented by Douglas Engelbart in 1964, the prototype featured two wheels that could roll in any direction and was connected to the computer via a cord. It was later refined and popularized by Xerox PARC in the 1970s, paving the way for the modern mouse we use today

    Fries, also known as French fries, chips, or finger chips, are thin strips of potato that have been deep-fried until crispy. They are a popular side dish or snack, often served hot and salted.   • :Pasta

    Pasta is a staple food of Italian cuisine, made from durum wheat flour mixed with water or eggs and formed into various shapes such as noodles, tubes, or sheets. It is typically boiled and served with a sauce, which may include ingredients such as tomatoes, meat, seafood, vegetables, or cheese. Pasta dishes are versatile and can be prepared in countless ways to suit different tastes.
